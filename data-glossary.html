Data Glossary

player_id: individual player identification id

seasons: number of seasons played (1985-2015)

all_star_seasons: number of all star selections for player (1985-2015)

all_star_percent: percentage of all star selections compared to number of seasons (1985-2015)

avg_yearly_salary: average yearly salary player was paid (1985-2015)

abs_per_double: numbers of at bats for player / number of doubles hit (1985-2015)

abs_per_sb: numbers of at bats for player / number of stolen bases (1985-2015)

abs_per_so: numbers of at bats for player / number of strike outs (1985-2015)

abs_per_bb: numbers of at bats for player / number of walks (1985-2015)

batting_avg: player's batting average (1985-2015)

abs_per_hr: numbers of at bats for player / number of home runs hit (1985-2015)
