Summary: All Star Pct & Batting Average

Perhaps the most basic indicator of a good hitter, this data shows Major League Baseball players' overall batting average (1985-2015) and how often that player was selected to an All Star Game in that span. While not always the case, there is a positive correlation with batting average and being an All Star selection. (Minimum 5 seasons played during this span, minimum 300 total at bats, only includes All Star selections, correlation coefficient = 0.452)
